ABOUT US

WHAT ARE WE

Welcome to ENCORE ESPORTS!

We are a passionate community of gamers from Jyothy Institute Of Technology. Our mission is to bring together gamers of all backgrounds and skill levels to celebrate our shared love for gaming.

Founded in 2023, our club is planning on organizing various gaming events, tournaments, and social gatherings. Whether you're into eSports, casual gaming, or just looking for a fun way to connect with fellow gamers, you've come to the right place!

Join us in exploring the latest games and re-living the nostalgia of old games, competing in exciting tournaments, and building lasting friendships within the gaming world. We look forward to having you as part of our gaming family!

RULES

ENCORE ESPORTS Rules and Regulations

1. Membership:

  • Eligibility: Membership is open to anyone interested in gaming.
  • Joining: To become a member, individuals must fill the form and pay the one time club membership fee.
  • Participation: Members are encouraged to attend club meetings and events regularly.

2. Meetings and Events:

  • Regular Meetings: Regular club meetings will be held for planning events and updating progress.
  • Announcements: Special events, tournaments, and gaming sessions will be announced in advance via whatsapp groups and in the announcements section on the website.
  • Member Involvement: Members are encouraged to suggest and organize gaming-related events.

3. Conduct and Behavior:

  • Respect: All members are expected to treat each other with respect and courtesy.
  • Zero Tolerance: Discrimination, harassment, bullying, or any form of disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Resource Usage: Members should be responsible and respectful when using club resources and gaming equipment.
  • Disciplinary Action: Any violations of these conduct rules will be addressed by club leadership and may result in disciplinary action, including suspension or expulsion.

4. Game Selection:

  • Focus: The club will primarily focus on playing hosting E-Games for its members and other interested gamers.
  • Member Input: Members are encouraged to propose new games for club consideration.
  • Decision Making: Selection of games for club activities will be decided by a majority vote among members.

5. Tournaments and Events:

  • Organizing Events: The club may organize gaming tournaments and events throughout the year.
  • Voluntary Participation: Participation in tournaments and events is voluntary.
  • Details: Entry fees, rules, and prizes for tournaments will be announced in advance.

6. Streaming and Content Creation:

  • Streaming and Content: Members are allowed to stream club activities or create gaming-related content.
  • Content Guidelines: Any content featuring the club must adhere to club rules and reflect a positive image of the club and its members.

7. Communication:

  • The club will use social media and the official website for announcements and discussions.
  • Members are encouraged to actively participate in club communication.

8. Amendments to Rules:

  • These rules and regulations may be amended by a majority vote of club members.
  • Members will be informed of any rule changes via Announcements.

9. Dispute Resolution:

  • Disputes among members will be resolved by club leadership through open communication and mediation.
  • In cases of severe rule violations or persistent misconduct, club leadership may take disciplinary action.

10. Acknowledgment and Agreement:

  • By becoming a member of ENCORE ESPORTS, individuals acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to abide by these rules and regulations.
